    First Visit

    On your first visit to Dental Dynamics your dentist will:

  • Explain the course of treatment clearly and listen carefully to your questions.
  • Check for decay, gum disease, oral cancer and jaw function.
  • Review your medical history and current medications.
  • Take any necessary dental x-rays.
  • Discuss the importance of routine dental checkups and care.

    • Regular dental care is an important step toward an attractive healthy smile. We welcome you to the Dental Dynamics family. We look forward to serving you.

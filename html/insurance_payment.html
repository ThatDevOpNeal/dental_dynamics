Insurance And Payment Options

Insurance

We accept most dental insurance plans. Our professional business staff will explain to you if we honor your insurance plan. We will also help file most insurance claims for you and we will save you time by contacting your insurance company directly.

No Insurance

If you do not have dental insurance you will immediately qualify for coverage with our Dental Dynamics plan. Our plan features:

  • Two routine cleanings, including exams and necessary x-rays.
  • 20% off all dental work, including cosmetic dentistry.
  • No deductible.
  • No exclusion for pre-existing conditions.

Contact us for more information at (847) 329-9801

Payments

We have numerous ways to make any dental treatment affordable for you. We offer these payment options:

  • Cash or Personal Check
  • Credit Card
  • 0% Financing (ask for details)
  • Other financial arrangements.(Ask for details when you visit the office)
